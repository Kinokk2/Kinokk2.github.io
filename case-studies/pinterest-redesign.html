Pinterest Sections

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Redesigning sections for better pin organization

Pinterest is a leading visual discovery platform where users save and organize inspiration. They wanted to redesign their sections feature to improve accessibility and reduce interface clutter.

The main challenge was streamlining navigation patterns to make organizing pins more intuitive while maintaining the platform's visual-first approach.

ROLE

Research & Discovery

Understanding user organization patterns

Through user interviews and analytics, we identified that users struggled with accessing their sections quickly. The feature was buried in navigation, leading to reduced usage and frustration with pin organization.
